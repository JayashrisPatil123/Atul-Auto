Driving Excellence Since 1970’s

Atul Auto Ltd. is one of India’s fastest growing three-wheeler companies, based in Rajkot, Gujarat. The company is known for its wide range of auto rickshaws catering to both passenger and cargo segments, powered by CNG, LPG, petrol, and diesel. Atul Auto has built a strong domestic presence with a network of dealerships and service stations, and is expanding its footprint in international markets as well.

Atul Auto is committed to providing reliable and fuel-efficient mobility solutions while supporting thousands of livelihoods across the country.

About Atul Auto

An Inception of Remarkable Legacy

The company’s origins can be traced back to 1970 when the Maharaja of Jamnagar tasked Late Shree Jagjivanbhai Karsanbhai Chandra with finding a solution for inoperative golf carts. Recognizing an opportunity to address the region’s growing transportation challenges, he ingeniously repurposed the carts’ engines to power an innovative three-wheeled vehicle, known as “Chhakada” among Saurashtra natives.

Glimpse of Our Manufacturing Facility

Atul Auto Limited prides itself on its cutting-edge manufacturing facilities situated in Shapar, Rajkot | Bhayla, Ahmedabad District. Our state-of-the-art plant boasts a robust production capacity of 1,20,000 vehicles annually.

This is how the small & innovative venture laid the foundation of Atul Auto Limited..
